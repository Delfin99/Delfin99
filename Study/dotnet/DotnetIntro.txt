How to start "Visual Studio Code" 

- It is called ".NET Core" within Unix environment.

- First of all, makes a folder.

- Types 'dotnet new console' or 'dotnet-sdk.dotnet new console'.

- Then, it creates three files like obj folder, Program.cs and vscode.csproj.

- To execute C# file(.cs), types 'dotnet run' or 'dotnet-sdk.dotnet run'.

- The result should be come out with creating bin folder.
